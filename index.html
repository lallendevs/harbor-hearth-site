Our Menu

Coastal Greek plates, baked fresh daily with locally sourced ingredients. Gluten-free and vegetarian options marked below.

Starters

  • Taramasalata

    A creamy dip made from salted cod roe, olive oil, and lemon juice, served with warm pita bread.

  • Grilled Halloumi

    Thick slices of halloumi cheese grilled and drizzled with olive oil and herbs.

  • Calamari

    Lightly breaded and fried calamari, served with lemon aioli.

Salads

Add to any salad: Grilled Chicken Breast | Grilled Shrimp | Grilled Sea Bass

Served as side or entree portion.

  • Aegean Salad

    A mix of cucumbers, tomatoes, red onion, and feta cheese, dressed with oregano vinaigrette.

  • Greek Village Salad

    A rustic salad of ripe tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, red onion, olives, and feta cheese, drizzled with olive oil.

  • Traditional Greek Salad

    Romaine, cucumber, tomatoes, onions, feta, Kalamata black olives, tossed in a lemon-oregano dressing.

Mains

  • Grilled Octopus

    Marinated octopus grilled and served with lemon potatoes and seasonal vegetables.

  • Shrimp Saganaki

    Shrimp, mussels, and calamari cooked in a spicy tomato sauce, topped with feta cheese.

  • Yemista

    Stuffed peppers and tomatoes with a savory mixture of rice, herbs, and spices.

  • Sea Bass (Lavraki)

    Whole grilled sea bass, olive oil, lemon and herbs.

Desserts

  • Baklava

    Flaky pastry filled with walnuts, pistachions, and honey syrup.

  • Greek Yogurt with Honey & Walnuts

    Thick strained yogurt drizzled with local honey and topped with toasted nuts.

  • Galaktoboureko

    Custard-filled filo with citrus syrup.

Beverages

  • Ouzo

    Traditional anise aperitif.

  • House White & Red Wine

    Selected from local vineyards.

  • Greek Coffee

    Strong, unfiltered brew served sweet or bitter.

  • Fresh House Lemonade

    Refreshing lemonade made with fresh lemons and a hint of mint.
